Recent client history

  • 2015 - 2016

    The Weekly with Charlie Pickering

    I produced weekly show graphics for both seasons 1 & 2 of ABC’s The Weekly with Charlie Pickering, along with additional graphics and motion graphics for longer pre-recorded segments. As it was a news show and very topical, work was completed within tight deadlines to be as current as possible.

  • 2014

    Spicks n Specks

    I designed and created the opening title sequence for the re-imagined Spicks n’ Specks. Additionally I created title cards for game segments and graphics for weekly topics.

  • 2013

    Adam Hills in Gordon St Tonight

    I assisted in the Adam Hills In Gordon St Tonight show branding & graphics over two seasons - this encompassed everything from motion graphics for broadcast, to print for large scale applications such as banners and outdoor marketing collateral.
